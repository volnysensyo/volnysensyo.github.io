Solutions
and Services

  • Metal Surface Finishing
    Solutions

    We are specializing in Bright and Semi-Bright Nickel Plating, Bright and Matte Tin Plating, Hard Chrome Plating, Nickel Chrome Plating and Electroless Nickel Plating for products of different sizes ranging from a size of a bottle cap to almost two meters in atleast one dimension.

    We accept requests for surface finishing, as well as for polishing only.

  • Development of
    New Functional
    Plating Technologies

    We work in-house on new functional plating processes, that meet our clients needs and contribute to the industry.

  • Consulting Services
    Regarding Surface Finishing
    Solutions

    We believe that plating should be taken in account from the start of product planning. Therefore we provide consulting to the clients who have just an idea, as well as to these who already have a product.
